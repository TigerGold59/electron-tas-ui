Overview

By using the in-app File → Export Existing Script or any of the options that include exporting, you can quickly and easily transfer TAS script files from your computer to your Switch. This requires a few things, though:

1. You must have sys-ftpd-light installed and enabled, a sysmod for your Switch that turns it into an FTP server. This is needed so that your computer can exchange files.
2. Your Switch must be on the same Wi-Fi network as your computer.
3. You must have a folder called "scripts" on the main directory of your SD card.

Once you have met these requirements, go to the Settings → Internet menu on your Switch. Scroll down and find the IP address field. This is what you must input on the "Switch's local IP address" box in the export window. From here, you can export and the app will backup whatever files it overwrites, in case you need them later.

NOTE: Your Switch's IP address will probably change after restarting, and will change any time you switch Wi-Fi networks.
